About Us

Our Mission

The pandemic has left many feeling lonely and displaced, especially for those who are unable to keep up with this rapidly advancing technological world. Our mission is to provide an easily accessible virtual platform specifically catered towards the interests and needs of senior citizens so that they can find companionship in a quarantine world without feeling left behind.

How It Works

Based on your responses and a dynamic database, our experts use the data collected from others like you who are seeking companionship to find the best candidates to be your new friend! Those who most match your interests and lifestyle are revealed after completion of the questionnaire, providing you the opportunity to get in contact with them and form new bonds. Your information will then be submitted to the database, providing others to reach out to you as well.
